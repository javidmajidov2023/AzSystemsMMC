"AzSystems” LLC operates in the Republic of Azerbaijan. One of the main goals of our company is the integration of rapidly developing information technologies and other innovations in the Republic of Azerbaijan and their simultaneous implementation. Despite the fact that the company is new, it has gathered highly trained professionals working in various fields around it. Our company regularly provides employees with courses, seminars and trainings on the development of information technologies held both in Azerbaijan and abroad in order to increase their professionalism. Our company already implements its experience and business activities in a number of state-important structures. "Azsystems" LLC operates There are several directions:

⦁ • Camera-video surveillance systems
⦁ • Security systems
⦁ • Telecommunications and information technologies
⦁ • Creation and support of various types of networks
⦁ • Setting up server rooms
⦁ • Installation of bollard systems
⦁ • Control release systems
⦁ • Medical equipment
⦁ • Fire control and security systems
⦁ • Sales and technical support of notebook, computer and UPS equipment
⦁ • All kinds of programming and more.

Since most of the equipment offered by our company is purchased directly from the manufacturing plants on the basis of contract and partnership, the equipment offered by us is highly selected in terms of quality, longevity and, most importantly, finance. We are not just trying to sell our products, we are trying to find the most optimal solution in terms of price/quality ratio. We are a team that is fully responsible to the client for the execution of works and we manage the deadlines of the works in a professional manner. At the same time, we have a strong material and technical base to implement large projects.

Strategy:

The main strategy of "Azsystems" LLC is to join the technologically leading companies in the Republic of Azerbaijan and the Caucasus region and contribute to the science of Azerbaijan with innovative projects.

Our mission:

One of our missions is to deliver technological processes that are developing rapidly in the world in a timely, correct and professional manner to the Azerbaijani public and mainly to the youth of Azerbaijan, and to study and integrate these processes.
